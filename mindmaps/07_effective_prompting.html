The Prompt Formula — Task · Context · Format
Three components that turn vague requests into clear insights
T
Task
"Analyse this customer feedback dataset and identify the top 5 issues"
C
Context
"This is a survey from 500 users collected after our product launch in Q3"
F
Format
"Return top 5 insights in bullet points with 2-sentence explanation each"

Common Prompting Mistakes
What kills output quality before you start
!
No context: "Analyse this dataset" with no background leaves Claude guessing
!
No format: you get an essay when you wanted 3 bullet points
!
No role: assigning a persona ("you are a senior analyst") dramatically improves output depth
!
Restarting instead of refining: select the specific part to improve and reply to that section
