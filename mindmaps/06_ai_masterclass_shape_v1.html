The SHAPE Framework — Your 2027 Survival Blueprint
5 principles to stay employed, relevant, and irreplaceable in an AI-driven economy
S
SOP-Driven
Document everything. As AI automates technical tasks, managing repeatable SOPs becomes your primary organisational value.
H
Human-in-Loop
Never treat AI output as the finish line. Validate every result, exercise judgment, and build on top with human intelligence.
A
Arbitrage
AI skills have a 3-month half-life. You're in constant learning mode. Master how to learn difficult foundational concepts fast.
P
Productivity
Building is no longer the moat — AI replicates it. Your edge is Trust & Distribution that AI cannot buy.
E
Employability
Focus on high-level problem solving. Resist intellectual obesity. Stay valuable by solving what AI can't autonomously handle.
