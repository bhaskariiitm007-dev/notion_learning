The SHAPE Framework — Your 2027 Survival Blueprint
5 principles to stay employed, relevant, and irreplaceable in an AI-driven economy
S SOP-Driven
Document everything. As AI automates technical tasks, managing repeatable SOPs becomes your core organisational value.
H Human-in-Loop
AI is a "drunk intern". Never treat output as the finish line. Validate, exercise judgment, and always build on top.
A Arbitrage
AI skills have a 3-month half-life. You're in constant learning. Master how to learn difficult foundational concepts fast.
P Productivity
Building is no longer the moat — AI replicates it instantly. Your edge is Trust + Distribution that no AI can buy.
E Employability
Focus on high-level problem solving. Resist intellectual obesity. Stay valuable by solving what AI can't handle alone.
