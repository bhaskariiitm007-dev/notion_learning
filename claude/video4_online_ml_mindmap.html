What is Online Learning?

4 Key Risks
Online learning is powerful but dangerous
1
Catastrophic forgetting — overwrites old patterns as it adapts
2
Data poisoning — adversaries inject corrupted records into live stream
3
Monitoring complexity — requires real-time drift and quality tracking
4
Infrastructure cost — Kafka + Flink + serving layer = high ops overhead
