Common Pitfalls
What causes burnout & information overload
!
Trying to read everything — 100s of papers daily. Impossible target = guaranteed burnout.
!
Chasing every new model — GPT-5, Claude, Gemini… evaluating each is a full-time job.
!
Comparing to Twitter highlights — you only see others' wins, not their hours of struggle.
!
No system → random consumption — without a rhythm, learning is reactive and forgettable.
